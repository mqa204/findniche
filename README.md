Having data is one thing. Knowing what to do with it is another. FindNiche hands you a massive database, but if you're randomly clicking through trending products hoping something sticks, you're wasting time.

Start by identifying niches with consistent demand rather than flash-in-the-pan trends. Use the platform's category analysis to see which product categories are growing steadily rather than spiking and crashing. Look for items with at least a few months of positive sales momentum. If a product suddenly appeared two weeks ago and is already declining, that's a red flag.
